# Visual Direction

## Intent

Moving Again should feel like a long-running Australian interstate removals
brand: plain-spoken, family-owned, practical, and quick to quote. The public
site uses a dark shell, deep red action colour, yellow accent, and direct
"Moving Made Simple. Since 1995." language.

The Moveroo subdomain should inherit those recognisable cues without copying
the public homepage layout or making the quote product feel heavy.

## Core Look

- **Mood:** dependable, established, fast, direct, Australia-wide.
- **Palette:** dark charcoal structure, deep red primary actions, yellow
  secondary accent, white or near-white form surfaces, and restrained grey
  borders.
- **Primary dark:** `#191919`.
- **Action red:** `#800006`.
- **Action red hover/accent:** `#B23850`.
- **Accent yellow:** `#F1C40D`.
- **Paper:** `#FAFAFA`.
- **Typography:** Roboto for body, navigation, headings, and compact form UI.
  Use bold or black weights for headings and CTAs.
- **Logo cue:** the circular silver/red arrow mark from `public/logo.svg`
  should identify the shell when space allows. Use a compact `MA` text mark
  only where the full mark cannot fit.
- **Motifs:** simple route lines, moving truck, boxes, or transport icons may
  support the quote context, but should stay secondary to the form.

## Subdomain Direction

- Keep the app layout and navigation generic to Moveroo's imported subdomain
  system.
- Use a light, fast form surface by default so the first question is reachable
  quickly on mobile.
- Use a branded dark header and footer; a compact dark hero or intro band is
  appropriate when it does not push the first form step down.
- Use red for primary CTAs, progress fill, selected choices, field focus, and
  validation emphasis.
- Use yellow as the secondary CTA/accent colour, not as the dominant form
  background.
- Keep household/removals quote as the primary journey, while leaving vehicle
  quote, booking, contact, and customer login available.

## What To Avoid

- Do not copy Backloading Removals black/red styling unless the Moving Again
  brand evidence supports it. This site has its own dark, deep-red, yellow,
  and Roboto profile.
- Do not recreate the public homepage hero in the quote app.
- Do not use large image-heavy heroes or decorative panels that delay the
  first mobile form step.
- Do not place route, truck, or box motifs behind input fields.
- Do not expose admin or provider links on public quote surfaces.
- Do not make customer login compete with the primary quote action in the
  header.

## Reusable Asset Families

- `logo`: silver/red arrow mark for compact shell identification.
- `route`: interstate route line or pin motif for national coverage.
- `boxes`: small service or reassurance iconography for household moves.
- `vehicle`: secondary motif for the vehicle quote journey.

## Approval Checklist

- The first form step is quickly reachable on mobile.
- Header, CTA, progress, selected-choice, focus, validation, and footer states
  use Moving Again tokens.
- Household quote is the primary journey.
- Vehicle quote, booking, contact, and customer login remain available through
  bounded public routes.
- Customer login is available but not promoted above the quote action.
- Provider and admin links remain disabled.
