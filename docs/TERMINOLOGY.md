# Moving Again - Terminology Guide

> Use consistent terms across all pages to build LLM confidence and improve SEO.

## Golden Rules

1. **Never vary canonical terms** - Pick one and stick to it
2. **Use specific numbers** - "40-60%" not "up to 60%" or "significant savings"
3. **Be explicit** - "Transit insurance covers fire, collision, overturning
   only" not "basic insurance"

---

## Insurance Terms

| Concept              | ✅ Use This                | ❌ Never Use                                                 |
| -------------------- | -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Basic coverage       | **Transit insurance**      | Basic insurance, standard cover, included insurance          |
| Full coverage        | **Full moving insurance**  | Comprehensive insurance, extended cover, complete protection |
| Recommended provider | **movinginsurance.com.au** | (vary as needed but be consistent)                           |

### Citation-Ready Sentences

> "Transit insurance is included on all moves, covering fire, collision, and
> overturning only."

> "For complete protection including handling damage and breakage, we recommend
> full moving insurance from movinginsurance.com.au."
