# Moving Again Homepage Audit

## Current Homepage Shape

The homepage in `src/pages/index.astro` is a custom commercial page worth preserving.

Key sections currently present:

1. hero centered on family-owned interstate moving since 1995
2. primary quote CTA into the removalist quote flow
3. trust indicators for insurance, family ownership, and weekly nationwide services
4. service cards for backloading, car transport, and self-pack containers
5. proof band around years in business, move volume, and customer satisfaction
6. explanatory long-form section about backloading and interstate moves

## Messaging Anchors

- family-owned, Australian-operated positioning
- savings claim around backloading
- long operating history since `1995`
- broad interstate coverage and weekly services

## Things To Preserve

- the brand-led hero and years-in-business story
- the service split between backloading, car transport, and container options
- strong early CTA to the quote flow
- trust proof integrated into the homepage rather than isolated on a separate page

## Things To Improve Later

- reduce repetition of hard-coded quote URLs where practical
- continue centralizing repeated brand and CTA values into config/constants
- evaluate whether the current custom SEO layer should gradually move toward the newer fleet baseline
